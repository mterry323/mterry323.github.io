About This Project

Boyfriend of Steel is a fangame for the cultural landmark anime series, Neon Genesis Evangelion, which borrows from the premise of its officially licensed spinoff games, Girlfriend of Steel and Girlfriend of Steel 2nd. It is a project created by and for LGBTQ+ fans of the show which hopes to explore more in-depth the queer themes of the show which are often left underexplored in canon material. Despite having two canonically LGBT male characters within the text of the show and movies, Eva's marketing tends to dance around their queerness in favor of the classic "leave it up to interpretation" explanation. This fangame seeks to give LGBTQ+ fans the explicit representation they may hope to see.

This is a demo which spans one in-game school day and uses assets from the Girlfriend games. The final game will be created with Renpy and could potentially look and feel very different from this demo. This is simply a proof of concept hosted online for easy access.

Disclaimer: This demo assumes prior knowledge of Eva and thus will not explain lore in depth. Further, there will be spoilers. Eva is available on Netflix for streaming.
