The College Application Era

Here are a bunch of works from the same point of time in my life: during senior year of high school, during college application time. I have a lot of mixed feelings about a lot of these (and had a lot of mixed feelings at the time I made them). Looking back, a lot of these have to do with trying to be seen as a "real" artist (the definition of that seemed to be "artist who draws realism"). Looking back on these now, I think I've realized that's not what I enjoy. Life drawing is important, of course â€” but that's different from realism as an art style.
