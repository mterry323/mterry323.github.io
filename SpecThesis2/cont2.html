Contain #2

Age when created: 17
Medium: Photoshop
Tools used: Wacom Intuos 4
Personal satisfaction: 4/5

This was drawn as one of two responses I had to make for my application to Rhode Island School of Art and Design. The prompt was "contain." I wanted one of my responses to be more literal, and the other to be more figurative. This is the figurative one, viewing the body as a container for different emotions - the different colors. I still like the concept, but I think this piece was hurt by me trying to draw in realism for the sake of seeming like a "real" artist. I didn’t end up getting into RISD.
