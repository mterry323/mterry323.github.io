Servant of Evil

Age when created: 11
Medium: Paint.NET
Tools used: Mouse (I think)
Personal satisfaction: 2/5

This is the REALLY old version of a piece I redrew for a college app, in order to show my growth as an illustrator. I was eleven when I drew this. It’s fanart for a couple of old songs I really liked at the time, called Daughter / Servant of Evil. As proud as I am of how far I’ve come, I love looking back at this thing and laughing at it. Like. Why are their heads shaped like that. Why are their hands smaller than their eyes. Where are their eyebrows.
