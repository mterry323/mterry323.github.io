Contain #1

Age when created: 17
Medium: Photography
Tools used: Canon EOS Rebel T4, Stuff found in my room
Personal satisfaction: 1/5

This is the more literal half of two responses to the prompt "contain" for my RISD application. The other was more metaphorical. This one was meant to literally contain things that I felt represented me. I just put things I like in a shoebox and took a picture of it. I did do some Photoshop to bring out the colors and make things stand out, but I, in my infinite wisdom, deleted the retouched photo because I ended up not liking this piece as a whole as time went on. I guess that’s also indicative of the stress I was under to make things at the time.
