Servant of Evil (Redrawn)

Age when created: 17
Medium: Photoshop
Tools used: Wacom Intuos 4
Personal satisfaction: 5/5

This is actually a redraw of a really old piece I drew when I was around 11 years old. It’s fanart for a series of songs from my childhood, called Daughter / Servant of Evil. They’re about a tyrannical princess and a servant that dies for her when the kingdom turns on her. Spoilers I guess.

I was actually pretty scared to put this in my portfolio for any of my college applications, but I end up putting it on my application to Champlain College in a little town in Vermont. The hope was to show them how much I’d improved in seven years. Though I had to pass it off as just "a princess and her servant" without any reference to the songs because I was worried about getting flack for drawing fanart. I ended up getting accepted to Champlain though!
