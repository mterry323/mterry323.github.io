Hand Study

Age when created: 17
Medium: Charcoal
Tools used: Charcoal, poster paper
Personal satisfaction: 2/5

Here are a bunch of hands I drew in my high school art class. We had to draw our own hand in five different poses to learn to pay attention to small details. I probably wouldn’t put this in my portfolio now - not instead of something with more personal significance to me. But I do like how these hands turned out, and I’m glad I did them. I draw people a lot, so this helped me to improve.
